Severity guidance:

- Critical: blocks core use, causes data loss, severe trust issue, or major production breakage
- High: major friction or clear failure in an important flow
- Medium: noticeable issue with workaround or limited scope
- Low: polish issue, edge case, or minor inconsistency

## Improvement Opportunities

- Perceived performance and responsiveness

## Untested or Blocked Areas

- What is working well?
