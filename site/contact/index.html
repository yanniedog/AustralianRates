Correction policy

Data correction requests are assessed against source feeds and capture logs. Validated issues are queued for fix in the next ingestion cycle.

For urgent corrections, include screenshots or source URLs so the issue can be verified faster.

Scope and limitations

AustralianRates provides general product-reference information and does not offer personal lending advice, credit assistance, or broker representation.

Always confirm pricing, eligibility, and fees directly with the institution before acting.
