Privacy

Privacy policy

This page explains what information AustralianRates may process and how to contact us.

Data we may process

AustralianRates is designed to publish product-reference rate data. We do not require account creation for public browsing.

Operational logs may include technical request metadata such as timestamp, endpoint path, and error context needed for reliability and abuse prevention.

How data is used

Technical logs are used for monitoring uptime, debugging ingestion/API issues, and maintaining service integrity.

No personal financial profiling or tailored lending recommendations are provided by this site.

Data minimization and public content

The platform is built around public product-reference feeds, not consumer bank-account access. Public pages are intended to remain usable without account sign-up.

Published rate datasets focus on lender product attributes rather than personal borrower records.

Contact and requests

For privacy questions or correction requests, email support@australianrates.com.
