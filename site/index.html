Compare variable and fixed home loan interest rates from Australia's major banks including Commonwealth Bank (CBA), Westpac, NAB, ANZ, Macquarie, Bendigo Bank, Suncorp, Bankwest, ING, AMP, HSBC, UBank, St. George, Bank of Melbourne, Bank of Queensland (BOQ), and Great Southern Bank. Rates are collected daily from official Consumer Data Right (CDR) feeds and cover owner-occupied and investment loans, principal-and-interest and interest-only repayments, across all LVR tiers from under 60% to 95%. Use the Rate Explorer to browse and sort, the Pivot Table to aggregate and cross-tabulate, or the Chart Builder to visualise trends over time.

Select filters and a date range, then click Load. Max 10,000 rows.

Configure axes and click Draw Chart.
